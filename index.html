What is IO Sonata ?

IOsonata is an open-source library designed for fast and easy development of IoT Microcontroller Unit firmware. The library is optimized for both power efficiency and performance, crucial for IoT devices that often have limited power resources. It is also optimized and supports Bluetooth Low Energy firmware development, common in IoT devices.

Current Supported IDEs and Platforms

Although IOSonata supports multiple IDE/Compilers, Eclipse GCC is the prefered IDE for IOSonata. It is the standard for embedded software development. Eclipse is 100% free and the most flexible IDE. Like any other IDE that you are new to, Eclipse could be little overwhelming at first, but with its simple file and tools navigation, Eclipse quickly becomes intuitive.
